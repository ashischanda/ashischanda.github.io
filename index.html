Hi!

I am Ashis Chanda, a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer & Information Sciences in Temple University, PA, USA.

I am working in the Data Science lab of Temple University as a research assistant of Prof. Slobodan Vucetic. My research focuses on electronic patient health record and medical data analysis using NLP and deep learning models. I also worked in Fox Chase Cancer Center as a research associate where I build an interactive software to visualize medical code relationship for patient cohort identification.

On more technical terms, my research work focuses around applying techniques such as Text Mining, NLP, Deep learning, Machine Learning (ML), Data Visualization, and Data Mining. I am passionate to work on those fields to explore real life problems and build a professional career in the ML research fields.
